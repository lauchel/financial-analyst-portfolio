01 Three-Statement Model Scenario Analysis Variance Reporting
Amazon Music — Three-Statement FP&A Model
Integrated P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow model forecasting Amazon Music's financials through FY2027E. Includes live assumption toggles, bull/base/bear scenario analysis, automated variance reporting vs. budget and prior periods, and executive-ready summary output. Built to mirror corporate FP&A workflows — blue inputs, black formulas, documented assumptions.
Data: MIDiA Research · industry estimates · Amazon reports · FY2025E–FY2027E
02 Customer Growth Forecast Scenario Analysis Variance Tracker
Amazon Music — Customer Growth Forecast
Interactive FP&A model forecasting MAUs, paid subscribers, revenue, and unit economics (LTV/CAC) through FY2027E. Includes live assumption toggles, bull/base/bear scenario analysis, a controllership variance tracker with ⚠ auto-alerts, and competitive benchmarking vs Spotify and Apple Music.
Data: MIDiA Research · industry estimates · Amazon reports · FY2025E–FY2027E
03 Subscriber & Revenue Growth Real data VBA automation
Netflix — Subscriber & Revenue Growth
5-year financial analysis across subscriber growth by region (UCAN/EMEA/LATAM/APAC), ARPU trends, operating margin expansion from 18% to 26.7%, and guidance vs actual beats. Includes an Excel model, VBA automation suite with 4 macros, and competitive benchmarking vs Disney+ and Amazon Prime Video.
Source: Netflix Inc. 10-K annual reports, SEC EDGAR · FY2020–FY2024
04 Growth Analytics Real data Business memo
Spotify — Growth & Unit Economics
Subscriber growth, ARPU trend, LTV/CAC analysis, segment profitability, and a full 2-page business memo. Covers FY2020–FY2024 across premium vs ad-supported revenue, conversion rate decline, and Spotify's first full year of operating profitability.
Source: Spotify Technology S.A. SEC 6-K filings, FY2020–FY2024
05 SQL · Variance Analysis Real data
Spotify — Budget vs Actual Tracker
Quarterly guidance vs reported results across revenue, gross margin, operating income, MAUs, and premium subscribers. Built on a 5-table normalized SQLite database with variance queries including window functions, CASE WHEN, and beat/miss scorecard.
Source: Spotify Technology S.A. SEC 6-K filings, FY2024

07 Multi-Entity Consolidation FX Scenario Analysis Intercompany Reconciliation
Global Marketplace — Multi-Entity Consolidation Model
Multi-entity, multi-currency P&L roll up for a rare and used book marketplace operating across four countries. Includes live FX scenario toggles (strong/base/weak dollar), a blended take rate control, and a full intercompany elimination and reconciliation schedule tying entity-level books to the consolidated USD statement.
